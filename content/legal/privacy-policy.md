# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 5, 2026**

DealerAddendums LLC ("us", "we", or "our") operates the DealerAddendums platform at **app.dealeraddendums.com** and the **dealeraddendums.com** website (together, the "Service"). This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. Terms used here have the same meaning as in our Terms of Use.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We do not sell your personal information, and we share it only as described below.

## Information we collect

**Account and contact information.** When you sign up or are invited, we collect information needed to identify and contact you and to set up your account — such as your name, email address, phone number, dealership or company name, and business address.

**Dealership business data.** To provide the Service, we process the business data you submit or connect, including vehicle inventory, options and pricing, addendum, buyers-guide and label content, your dealership's branding, and the names and email addresses of the users you invite.

**Billing information.** Subscription and label charges are handled by our third-party payment processor. We receive billing details such as invoice history and plan status; we do not store full payment-card numbers on our systems.

**Sign-in credentials.** The Service supports passwordless sign-in. Depending on how you sign in, we process one-time email sign-in codes and/or passkey (WebAuthn) credentials, and a password if you choose to set one.

**Log data.** We collect information your browser or device sends when you use the Service ("Log Data"), which may include your IP address, browser type and version, the pages you visit, the date and time of your visit, time spent on pages, and similar diagnostics.

**Cookies.** Cookies are small data files that may include an anonymous unique identifier, sent from a website to your browser and stored on your device. We use cookies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand and improve how the Service is used. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when a cookie is sent; if you do, some features of the Service may not function, and we recommend leaving them enabled.

## Information we do not access or store

DealerAddendums is a vehicle-merchandising tool — not a deal, F&I, or payment system. We do **not** view, access, read, or store:

- **Your customers' personal information.** We do not receive the names, contact details, identification, financial details, or other personal information of the consumers who buy or inquire about your vehicles.
- **Sale or deal information.** We are not part of your deal, F&I, or sales-transaction flow, and we do not receive sale prices, buyer agreements, trade-in details, financing terms, or credit applications.
- **Credit-card or payment-card data.** Subscription and label payments are handled entirely by our PCI-compliant payment processor; full payment-card numbers are never transmitted to or stored on our systems.

The information we handle is limited to your vehicle inventory and pricing data, your dealership's branding and document content, and the account contact details of the users you invite (described above).

## How we use your information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve the Service; to authenticate you and keep your account secure (including passwordless sign-in); to communicate with you and respond to requests; to process subscriptions, orders, and label shipments; to provide customer support; to detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues; and to comply with our legal obligations.

We support the Do Not Track ("DNT") browser preference. You can enable or disable DNT in your web browser's settings.
